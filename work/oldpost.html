Oldpost

Branding, identity and design for a wonderful coffee house
No items found.

Wonderfully warm

Located on Nutfield High Street, this beautiful, character-filled building is a former Post Office that is now home to a unique mix of local businesses.

Our task was to create the identity for a new coffee house opening in Nutfield, Surrey. The aesthetic would need to pay homage to the building’s history as a former Post Office, whilst appearing welcoming and friendly to the local community.

‍

A red colour scheme was selected to reflect the building’s history and a letterbox shaped logo was created. The clear, concise branding is memorable, yet adaptable and flexible to the needs of the business thanks to its simple typography.

View Website

What next?

Oldpost is quickly becoming a well known name within the local area; the coffee house is a success and is going from strength to strength every month. We’re still working closely with Oldpost to create new and exciting material for their brand.
